Insights
Women dentists have more distractions than their men colleagues especially if they have family and children. It forces them to work part time. 
Dentists community is not as diverse as the US population. Language and cultural barriers increase disparities and can cause discrimination. This is evident among LGBT community. The hypothesis is; dentists from these communities understand/communicate with them better, and there is a higher chance that they go back to their hometown after graduation.
